Generative Design by Artificial Intelligence

Product

At present, Gigala software consists of two moduli: offshore dynamics and topology optimization. It uses artificial intelligence to assist an engineer in her design. You can use it as research or engineering analysis tool for different mechanical structures, riser systems, mooring systems, pipelay, renewables, etc.

The software is constantly being developed. Some models that it includes now are:

  • Topology optimization of structures
  • Topology optimization of parts
  • Pipelay 3D
  • Pipelay 2D
  • Pipelay profile optimization
  • Spool design

Mission

Solving creativity to advance science and engineering. Making AI accessible.

Technological explosion, meaning an exponential growth in the rate of scientific discoveries, will happen when everyone gets access to powerful AI which only few can afford these days. At Gigala, I am on the quest to make this happen sooner by applying artificial intelligence driven generative design to provide solutions in science and engineering. Currently, I focus on the reinforcement learning guided design of structural and electrical elements and components, and offshore dynamics. But I am constantly expanding into the new ways that AI can be applied to STEM.

FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

How can I participate in the project?

You can contact me directly via support link at the top or you can send a pull request to the Gigala github repository.

What do I get as a sponsor?

You will get an ability to influence some future features of the product.

What is an intended audience for your software?

Students, engineers, and researchers. People who want to learn more about new ways to combine AI and engineering analysis.

Is it more a research devein product or commercially oriented one?

It is kind of both. I believe that no modern product can be developed without a cutting edge research. But I do have intentions to commercialize it at some point down the road. Hence, I try to approach the development process as both industry and academic standards.

Does it have a GUI?

No. Currently, my software has basic visualization only but, in the future, I intend to integrate it with FreeCAD and ParaView.

How does your product differ from others?

It is an artificial intelligence driven product. I combine it with classical engineering methods, but AI comes first. For example, I will include topology optimization by reinforcement learning, automated logic proving, physics informed neural networks and many more in my software.
